Gallery of Great Indian Temples

Meenakshi Temple

Located in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, this temple is dedicated to Goddess Meenakshi and Lord Sundareswarar.

Brihadeeswarar Temple

A UNESCO World Heritage Site in Thanjavur, Tamil Nadu, known for its stunning architecture.

Jagannath Temple

Located in Puri, Odisha, this temple is famous for its annual Rath Yatra (chariot festival).

Somnath Temple

Situated in Gujarat, it is one of the twelve Jyotirlinga shrines of Lord Shiva.

Tirupati Balaji Temple

Located in Tirumala, Andhra Pradesh, it is one of the wealthiest temples in the world.

Konark Sun Temple

A 13th-century temple in Odisha, designed as a giant chariot dedicated to the Sun God.
